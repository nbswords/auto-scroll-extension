Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

Auto Scroll is a browser extension that automatically scrolls web pages at a user-defined speed with configurable pause intervals. Your privacy is important to us, and this extension is designed with a privacy-first approach.

Data Collection

Auto Scroll does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history or page content is accessed or recorded
  • No cookies or tracking mechanisms are used
  • No data is sent to any external server
  • No analytics or telemetry of any kind is implemented

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • activeTab — To inject the scroll script into the currently active tab when you click the extension icon
  • scripting — To execute the auto-scroll content script on the active page
  • Content Scripts (all URLs) — To enable the scroll functionality to work on any web page you choose to use it on

Local Storage

The only data stored locally is your theme preference (light/dark mode), saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the extension's GitHub repository.
